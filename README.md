Hi, I'm Evan 👋

I'm a Senior Associate at JPMorganChase in AI, Automation & Data Strategy, and a part-time MSc Computer Science student at the University of Bath. My work sits at the intersection of AI deployment, evaluation, and governance — and I'm building toward a research career in clinical AI.

What I work on

At JPMorganChase, I design and deploy LLM evaluation frameworks in a regulated financial environment — building systems to assess whether AI models are reliably doing what they claim to do in high-stakes contexts. I work across data science, engineering, and business teams to get AI into production safely.
